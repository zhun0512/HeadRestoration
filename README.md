# Head Restoration - HITMAN (2016) and HITMAN 2 Head
[Mod Manager Download](https://hitman-resources.netlify.app/smf-install-link/https://github.com/zhun0512/HeadRestoration/releases/latest/download/mod.framework.zip) | [Manual Download](https://github.com/zhun0512/HeadRestoration/releases/latest/download/mod.framework.zip)

Restores Agent 47's head from HITMAN (2016), HITMAN 2 (2018) and Closed Alpha (2015).

## Intro

I've always been a fan of 47's look in the HITMAN 2016 pre-rendered cutscenes. 

Since there wasn't a mod capable of a seamless Integration of 47's appearance back to 2016  .
So I decided to take matters into my own hands and restore 2016 head model for WoA.
I also change UI elements resemble 2016 face for consistency.

What started as a personal project has grown significantly. Due to the number of requests to bring back 47's look from other entries, I've also restored head model from HITMAN 2 and Closed Alpha (2015).

Provide a variety of legacy head models for players to choose from.
These model fully customizable. capable for mix and match, with mod support to suit your desired look of 47.
Addon for Peacock Server support is also available.

This mod improved upon base of these modder's work. credits for MLuka47, musicalmushr00m, Duckilous, thanks to them and their amazing work.
